ðŸ“Ž Security Questions

Question: What is security questions?

Answered: You will see this screen when you are either creating a new account or if you forgot your password.

Creating a new account:

If you are creating a new account you will have to chose 3 different questions & answers to recover your account, in case you loose your account. Please write down these questions & answers somewhere. Then click continue.

Forgot password:

If you are forgot your password and want to reset it, first thing you will have to provide is, these questions answers. You can only reset your password if you answer all these questions correctly.

If there is an error or answers are incorrect you will see it at the bottom status bar.

Question: How do I create new password?

Answered: To create new password you first have to follow steps on 'How to reset password?' under login page.

You can now create new password! Here you need to satisfy some requirements you can find these below.

Requirements

Your password is at least 8 character long.

Your password contains at least 1 upper case character and 1 lower case character.

Your password contains at least 1 special character. e.g.( &,$,#,@,*)
